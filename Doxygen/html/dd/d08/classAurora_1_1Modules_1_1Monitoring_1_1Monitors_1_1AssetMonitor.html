Number of persistent requests for missing textures we have started blocking from clients. To some extent this is just a temporary statistic to keep this problem in view - the root cause of this lies either in a mishandling of the reply protocol, related to avatar appearance or may even originate in graphics driver bugs on clients (though this seems less likely).
